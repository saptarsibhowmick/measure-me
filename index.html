Your bespoke
measurement session

Stand in front of any camera. The AI detects your body landmarks and derives all 14 measurements from your tailor brief. No data leaves your device.

  • 1Wear only briefs, socks and shoes. Clothing adds bulk and skews measurements.
  • 2Prop your device 2–3 metres away at hip height — a ledge, tripod, or stack of books works well.
  • 3Stand in front of a plain wall with good even lighting. Avoid backlighting.
  • 4Keep arms slightly away from your sides. Stand naturally erect.
  • 5You will capture two poses: front view, then right-side view.

Privacy: All AI processing runs entirely on this device using Google MediaPipe BlazePose. No images, landmarks, or measurements are transmitted anywhere.
