v2 uses two AI models: BlazePose for body landmarks and Depth Anything V2 for per-pixel depth. Together they measure body thickness directly — no side view needed.

  • 1Wear only briefs, socks and shoes — clothing adds bulk
  • 2Prop device 2–3 m away at hip height — one front pose only
  • 3Stand against a plain, evenly-lit wall — avoid mirrors and glass
  • 4Arms slightly away from sides, natural upright stance
  • 5After capture, depth AI runs for 5–15 seconds — stay on the page

Privacy: both AI models run entirely on this device via WebGPU or WebAssembly. No images, landmarks, or depth maps are transmitted anywhere.
